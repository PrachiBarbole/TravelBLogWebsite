Lonavala

Lonavala is one of the twin hill stations - Lonavala and Khandala (these two can be easily visited together). The popular tourist attractions in Lonavala are Bhaja caves, Bushi dam, Karla caves .

DELLAS ADVENTURE PARK:- Dellas Adventure Park is an amusement park and the best place to visit in Lonavala in one day. It consists of activities such as Aqua Zorb, Swoop Swing, Flying Fox, Rappelling, Buggy Ride, Land Zorbing, Paintball, etc. The park also organizes nightclubs with several cool restaurants for fine dine and binge eating. Enjoy your adventurous trip to Lonavala exclusively at Della Adventure Park with your friends, group and family.

ADLABS IMAGICA:- Adlabs Imagica was opened in 2013. It is one of the best entertainment theme parks located on the Mumbai-Pune Expressway near Khopoli. It covers a vast area of 300 acres with a magnificent infrastructure. The park consists of three entertainment zones, namely Theme Park, Snow Park and Water Park with plenty of rides and roller coasters, and a water park with various rides. It is one of the best places to visit in Lonavala for couples who want to enjoy rides and make their travel adventurous.

BHUSHI DAM:- Lonavala is built on the Indrayani River and is one of the most popular and best places to visit or travel around. Bhushi Dam is one of the picturesque spots, and the water running down the dam makes it look stunning down the monsoon. Although jumping in the dam is restricted, the luxurious greens, rugged terrains, and chilly water attract crowds when the rain is pouring down.

BHAJA CAVES:-It is one of the oldest caves in India. It is also classified as a national monument of India, as it belongs to the First and Second Century BCE. The caves are over 2200 years old.

KARLA CAVES :- They are close to Bhaja Caves and are one of India's most popular tourist attractions. When you plan a vacation in Maharashtra, make sure that you visit Karla Caves in your stay, as it is one of the finest places well preserved Hinayana Buddhist Chaitya in India. Karla's caves are similar to Bhaja Caves, as it is one of the oldest caves backdated in the 2nd and 5th century BC.

RAJMACHI FORT:-The most popular place to visit in Lonavala is Rajmachi Fort. One of the best places which offer stunning scenery. The Best time to visit is during the rainy season. It offers magnificent views of the Western Ghats and Shirota Dam. Some vike freaks ride up to the fort as it rains. Those who love trekking and hiking can visit this place during the monsoon season.
