Welcome to Manali

Where you leave manali,you carry if with you wherever you go.

Hadimba Temple

Also known as Hidimba Devi Temple and Dhungari Temple. Maharaja Bahadur Singh built this ancient shrine in 1553.

Solang Valley

Solang valley is a paradise for adventure and sports lovers. While you are here, you can test your skills with a pair of skis or explore by snowmobile. When the weather warms up, people flock to zorbing and paragliding.

Jogini falls

The precipitous falls of the Jogini falls are a short and difficult walk away from the main city of Manali.

The Manali Gompa

The ornate design and quintessential Tibetan architecture make Gadhan Thekchhokling Gompa, better known as Manali Gompa, one of the best sites in Manali.

Arjun Gufa (Arjun cave), Manali

Located near the dizzying currents of the Beas River, the Arjun cave is associated with a legendary history of the Mahabharata Hindu epic.

Van Vihar

If you are looking for moments of tranquility in the bohemian surroundings of the city of Manali, a short walk in Van Vihar Park is recommended.
