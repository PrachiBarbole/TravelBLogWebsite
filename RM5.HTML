Maldives

The journey time to The Maldives varies from two and a half hours, for direct flights, to almost 10 hours for flights that have long layovers...

MALE:- The capital city, also known as the "King's Island". It's a bustling city with many attractions, including the National Museum and Artificial Beach.

ADDU ATOLL:- The southernmost atoll in the Maldives, with many islands including Gan, Meedhoo, Hulhudhoo, Hithadhoo, Maradhoo, and Feydhoo. It's a popular destination for diving, snorkeling, and fishing.

UTHEEMU ISLAND:- Home to the historical landmark Utheemu Ganduvaru, a wood structure surrounded by tall trees.

VAADDHOO ISLAND:- Known for its glowing beach, which is caused by bioluminescence, a phenomenon where tiny planktons emit light to defend themselves from fish.

HURAWALHI ISLAND:- Home to the 5.8 Undersea Restaurant, where diners can enjoy seafood and watch sharks and other sea animals swim past.

NORTH MALE ATOLL:- Home to the Lion's Head and Wattaru Kandu dive sites, where divers can see reef sharks, stingrays, and garden eels.

LANKANFINUKO:- Also known as Manta Point, this is a great place to see manta rays being cleaned by cleaner fish.
